Find your neighbourhood to stay in Amsterdam!

Airbnb clusters

The red-to-green background overlay represents clusters of areas with similar price-per-capita of the Airbnb apartments. The colour goes from green to red as the mean price of the apartments in the cluster increases.

Tweet clusters

The donut-shaped markers represent the sentiments of clusters of tweets in the neighbourhood. The colours represent respectively a positive, neutral and negative sentiment of the tweets.

Word Cloud

Clicking a point in the map will reveal a word cloud containing the most popular words used in the tweets in the shown radius.
